2027: Race to AGI! is a satirical game about the wild world of Artificial General Intelligence research: where good intentions meet impossible choices, and the future of humanity is at stake!

You play the fresh-faced CEO of OpenBrain, a leading AI lab.

THRILL! as you navigate the complex maze of trade-offs between progress and safety.
LAUGH! as you fend off moral quagmires, irresponsible competitors, and rogue nation states.
CRY! as you make impossible decisions that will nevertheless probably shape the future of humanity.

Will you prioritize alignment over speed? Can you maintain public trust while pushing the boundaries of what's possible? Just like in real life, every choice in 2027: Race to AGI! has consequences. The clock is ticking, and the race is on!



Inspired by the AI 2027 paper, Situational Awareness, Reigns, Democratic Socialism Simulator, Wingspan, and Papers, Please.
